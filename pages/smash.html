Smash.com

Co-founder of the fast, secure file transfer platform.

As co-founder of Smash.com, Haro Gharbigi helped build one of the leading file transfer platforms, enabling millions of users worldwide to send large files quickly, securely, and without size limits.

Smash.com addresses a universal problem: the need to share large files without the constraints of email attachments or complicated upload processes. The platform combines speed, security, and simplicity to deliver a seamless file transfer experience.

Co-founding Smash.com provided valuable experience in scaling consumer-facing technology products and building systems that handle massive data volumes reliably. These infrastructure lessons directly inform current work in healthcare technology.

The venture demonstrates the power of solving focused, universal problems with elegant solutions. By removing friction from file sharing, Smash.com has become an essential tool for creative professionals, businesses, and individuals who need to move large files efficiently.

This entrepreneurial experience complements healthcare-focused work at AxiaASC, illustrating a broader commitment to building technology that makes complex processes simple and accessible.
