Gumroad

Digital product marketplace for resources and educational materials.

Gumroad serves as a marketplace for digital products and resources that package insights on healthcare technology, AI implementation, and business strategy into actionable formats.

Digital products on Gumroad represent distilled expertise from years of building technology businesses in healthcare. These resources provide frameworks, templates, and guidance that readers can immediately apply.

Unlike free content that provides general insights, Gumroad products dive deeper into specific topics with comprehensive coverage designed to deliver concrete results for purchasers.

The platform enables direct creator-to-customer relationships, ensuring those who invest in these resources receive quality materials backed by real-world experience building AI solutions for medical facilities.

Products range from strategic guides for executives to practical toolkits for implementation teams, addressing various needs across the journey from AI awareness to successful deployment.
