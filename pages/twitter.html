X / Twitter

Real-time thoughts on technology trends and industry developments.

On X (formerly Twitter), I share immediate reactions to technology news, engage with industry conversations, and offer bite-sized insights on the intersection of artificial intelligence and healthcare.

The @harotweet handle serves as a real-time window into my thinking on emerging developments in healthcare technology. Unlike the more polished, long-form content on other platforms, Twitter captures thoughts as they develop, creating an authentic view of how I process industry changes.

Content ranges from commentary on breaking news in healthcare AI to observations about broader technology trends affecting business strategy. Occasional threads dive deeper into specific topics when the format suits the message.

Following @harotweet provides access to my curated stream of relevant content, including retweets of valuable perspectives from others in the space. It's a way to stay connected to the pulse of healthcare technology innovation as it happens.
