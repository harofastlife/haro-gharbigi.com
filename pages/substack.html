Substack

Newsletter publication delivering analysis on healthcare AI and entrepreneurship.

The Substack newsletter provides subscribers with regular, in-depth analysis of healthcare technology developments, AI implementation strategies, and lessons from the entrepreneurial trenches.

Substack offers a direct line to readers interested in serious examination of how artificial intelligence is transforming healthcare. Unlike social media snippets, the newsletter format allows for thorough exploration of complex topics that demand nuanced treatment.

Each edition combines industry analysis with practical guidance, drawing from hands-on experience building AI solutions for ambulatory surgery centers. Subscribers receive insights grounded in real-world implementation rather than theoretical speculation.

Topics range from tactical advice on technology adoption to strategic frameworks for healthcare leaders navigating digital transformation. The newsletter also includes occasional deep dives into specific AI applications and their implications for medical practice management.

By subscribing, healthcare executives, technology professionals, and fellow entrepreneurs join a community of readers committed to understanding and leveraging the potential of AI in medical settings. The discussion continues beyond individual newsletters through engagement with subscribers.
