Medium

Long-form articles exploring technology trends and business strategy insights.

Medium provides the ideal platform for sharing in-depth analyses of technology trends, particularly the practical application of artificial intelligence in healthcare environments and the strategic considerations business leaders must navigate.

Writing on Medium allows me to explore complex topics with the depth they deserve. Rather than surface-level observations, these articles dig into the real challenges organizations face when implementing new technologies, drawing from direct experience building AI solutions for ambulatory surgery centers.

Topics range from technical explorations of machine learning applications in medical settings to broader discussions about leadership during technological transitions. Each piece aims to provide actionable insights rather than abstract theory, helping readers understand not just what's possible but how to achieve it.

The healthcare technology landscape evolves rapidly, and Medium serves as a space for timely commentary on emerging developments. Whether examining new regulatory frameworks, breakthrough AI capabilities, or shifting market dynamics, these articles offer perspectives grounded in hands-on industry involvement.

Beyond technical content, I also share reflections on the entrepreneurial journey itself, discussing the lessons learned from nearly two decades of building businesses in technology sectors. These pieces resonate with fellow founders navigating similar challenges and opportunities.
