SoundCloud

Audio content and podcast appearances exploring entrepreneurship themes.

SoundCloud hosts audio content that allows listeners to engage with ideas about healthcare technology, entrepreneurship, and business strategy during commutes, workouts, or any time screen-free learning is preferred.

Audio format offers unique advantages for conveying ideas that benefit from conversational delivery. Discussions on SoundCloud capture the nuance and spontaneity that sometimes gets lost in written content.

Content includes reflections on building healthcare technology companies, insights from entrepreneurial experiences, and perspectives on emerging trends in AI implementation and business strategy.

The audio format reaches audiences who prefer listening over reading, expanding accessibility for busy professionals who want to stay informed while multitasking or during time that doesn't allow for reading.

SoundCloud's platform makes audio content easily shareable and embeddable, enabling distribution across multiple channels and integration with other content platforms.
