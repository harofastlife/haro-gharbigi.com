Instagram

Visual stories from the intersection of technology and daily life.

Instagram offers a more personal glimpse into life as a technology entrepreneur, capturing moments that tell the story behind the professional achievements and business ventures.

While most of my online presence focuses on professional insights and business content, Instagram provides space for the visual dimension of entrepreneurship. The feed captures everything from Los Angeles life to behind-the-scenes moments building healthcare technology solutions.

Photography has always been a way to process experiences and share perspectives beyond what words can convey. Through carefully curated images, followers get a sense of the environments, people, and moments that shape my work and worldview.

Content includes snapshots from industry events, glimpses of daily routines, travel experiences, and occasional reflections presented visually. Stories and reels provide more spontaneous updates that complement the more curated main feed.

For those interested in the human side of technology entrepreneurship, Instagram offers connection that complements the more business-focused content elsewhere. It's a reminder that innovation happens in the context of real life.
