About

A technology entrepreneur dedicated to revolutionizing healthcare through intelligent software solutions.

With nearly two decades immersed in the world of entrepreneurship and technology, I've channeled my passion into creating meaningful change in the healthcare sector. My journey has been defined by a singular focus: identifying where advanced technology can solve real problems for real people.

Based in Los Angeles, I lead AxiaASC, a company at the forefront of ambulatory surgery center technology. We're building AI-powered platforms that help medical facilities operate more efficiently while delivering exceptional patient experiences.

I believe the future of healthcare lies in the thoughtful application of artificial intelligence—not technology for its own sake, but solutions that genuinely improve outcomes and empower healthcare professionals to do their best work.
